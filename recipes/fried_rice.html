Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular dish in many cultures, featuring rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or large pan with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Push the rice to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs in the other side.
  3. Add the garlic and stir for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Mix everything together and cook for another minute or two until heated through.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
